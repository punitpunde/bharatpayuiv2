Fast Gas Booking Online at Paytm

A gas cylinder is one of the most important utilities for us. Whether it is for our home or our for commercial use such as in a restaurant. But who likes to go outside and stand in the queues for hours just to book a gas cylinder. Feel the comfort of your home and book cylinder without any hassles following a few easy steps with Paytm's Online LPG Gas Booking service for HP gas.

Advantages of Online LPG Gas Booking at Paytm

Online gas booking service of Paytm comes with many advantages which are as follows-
  • It is easy, convenient and gets processed within a minute
  • There are no hidden or additional charges
  • Transactions on Paytm are very safe and secure
  • You can book a gas cylinder from anywhere and anytime

    • Flexible payment methods

    Paytm provides you with many flexible payment methods for your online gas booking for HP Gas. You can pay the gas booking amount using a debit card, credit card, net banking, Paytm wallet and UPI (UPI is only available with Paytm app)

    How to Make Online Gas Booking Paytm?

    You can easily a cylinder at Paytm by following these simple steps-

  • Go to the gas booking page on Paytm
  • Select "Book a Cylinder" and select "HP Gas" as your operator
  • Enter your Consumer Number/Linked Mobile Number
  • Select your "Gas Agency"
  • Click on "Proceed"
  • Enter the booking amount
  • Proceed to pay with the payment mode of your preference
  • You are done with it
